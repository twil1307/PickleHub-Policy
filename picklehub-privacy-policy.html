Privacy Policy for PickleHub

Effective Date: July 28, 2025
Last Updated: July 28, 2025

PickleHub ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile app to discover pickleball courts and connect with other players.

By using the PickleHub app, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Facebook Login Information

When you log in using Facebook, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Facebook profile picture (avatar)
  • Your Facebook user ID (for linking purposes only)

We do not post on your behalf or access your full Facebook profile or friend list.

1.2 Optional User-Provided Data

You may optionally provide:

  • A short bio or skill level
  • Preferred playing times or locations
  • Contact preferences

1.3 Location Data

If you enable location access, we may collect your GPS coordinates to:

  • Show nearby pickleball courts
  • Recommend nearby users who are also looking for a match

We do not store precise location data beyond what is needed to perform these functions, unless explicitly permitted by you.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you via Facebook
  • To personalize your user experience
  • To help you discover nearby courts and connect with other players
  • To maintain your user profile within the app
  • To improve the app’s features and functionality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely in our backend services (e.g., Firebase), which employ industry-standard encryption and access control mechanisms.

Your Facebook user ID is stored only to maintain your identity across sessions. Other Facebook data (like name and email) is only stored if required for core functionality and is never shared outside the app.

3.1 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with the app's services. If you delete your account, we will delete your data as described in Section 4. We may retain some data for a limited period if required for legal or regulatory obligations, dispute resolution, or to enforce our agreements.

3.2 International Data Transfers

Your information, including personal data, may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those from your jurisdiction. By using PickleHub, you consent to such transfer of information.

4. Data Deletion

You may request to delete your data at any time by contacting us via email (see Section 9). Upon request, we will:

  • Delete your user profile from our database
  • Unlink your Facebook ID
  • Remove any optional data you’ve provided

5. Third-Party Services and Analytics

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore to manage authentication and store data. These services are provided by Google and adhere to strict privacy and security guidelines.

We may also use anonymized analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics) to understand app usage and improve features. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information.

We do not use third-party advertising services.

6. Limited Data Sharing

We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or a government agency).

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

7. Children’s Privacy

PickleHub is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., revoke location access or uninstall the app)

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you’d like to request data deletion, you may contact us at:

Email: support@picklehub.app
App Name: PickleHub
Platform: iOS and Android

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users via in-app alerts or email if major changes are made. Continued use of the app after such updates implies consent to the revised policy.
